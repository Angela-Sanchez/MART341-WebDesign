Types of Websites

Educational

Educational websites provide information for educational institutions or online courses.
They can also provide resources for educational materials.
Our UM website is an example.

Personal Blog

Personal blogs are a way for people to write about one particular topic that interests them.
They usually provide information as well as photos, sometimes they provide instructions.
Examples of blogs include travel, food, crafting, etc.

Business

Business websites provide information for small businesses or corporations.
They often provide links for viewing products, self-checkout, and payment.
They almost always provide information for the company as well.

Tone of Website

The tone of a website is important, as it conveys the message you would like to send based on your topic. For my website, I may use a playful tone.
